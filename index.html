Jollof Rice

A popular Nigerian dish made with rice cooked in a rich tomato and pepper sauce.

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 5 people

Difficulty: Medium

Dish Preview

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long grain rice
  • 4 fresh tomatoes
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Blend tomatoes, peppers, and onions into a smooth mixture.
  2. Heat oil and fry tomato paste for a few minutes.
  3. Add blended mixture and cook until thick.
  4. Add seasoning, spices, and water. Bring to boil.
  5. Add rice, stir, cover, and cook until soft.

Tips

For smoky flavor, allow slight burning at the bottom (party jollof style).
