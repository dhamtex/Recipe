Jollof Rice

A popular Nigerian dish made with rice cooked in a rich tomato and pepper sauce.

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 5 people

Difficulty: Medium

Dish Preview

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long grain rice
  • 4 fresh tomatoes
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Blend tomatoes, peppers, and onions into a smooth mixture.
  2. Heat oil in a pot and fry the tomato paste for a few minutes.
  3. Add the blended mixture and cook until it thickens.
  4. Add seasoning, spices, and water, then bring to a boil.
  5. Add washed rice, stir, cover, and cook until rice is soft.

Tips

For smoky flavor, allow the rice to slightly burn at the bottom (party jollof style).
